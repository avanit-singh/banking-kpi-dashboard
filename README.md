## Overview
Executive-level KPI dashboard for multi-branch banking operations.
Tracks CASA growth, loan disbursements, customer acquisition,
cross-sell performance, and revenue across 4 branches over 3 months.

---

## Key Analyses
1. CASA Growth Rate & Trend Analysis
2. Loan Disbursement Volume & Approval Rate KPIs
3. Customer Acquisition Rate Tracking
4. Product Penetration & Cross-Sell Performance
5. Branch Performance Scorecard
6. Revenue Growth (Month-on-Month)
